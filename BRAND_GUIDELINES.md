# Brand Guidelines

## Brand essence

- **Name:** Sessiono
- **Category:** Session musician collaboration platform
- **Voice:** Clear, collaborative, creator-first, trustworthy

## Messaging pillars

1. Finish songs faster with trusted collaborators
2. Keep music projects organized from brief to final stems
3. Protect both artists and session musicians with structured milestones

## Tone and copy rules

- Use plain language over jargon
- Prioritize confidence + clarity over hype
- Focus on outcomes: turnaround speed, quality, trust

## Visual direction (initial)

- Dark-forward UI with high contrast for long studio sessions
- Accent colors for project state (briefing, in-progress, revision, complete)
- Clean typography optimized for metadata-heavy workflows

## Naming conventions

- "Project Brief" = hiring request
- "Session Offer" = musician proposal
- "Milestone" = payable delivery checkpoint
- "Delivery Pack" = stems + bounce + notes

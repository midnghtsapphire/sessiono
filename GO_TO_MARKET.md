# Go-To-Market Plan (Sessiono)

## Problem

Independent artists often need reliable, fast session support (vocals, guitar, drums, keys, production edits, mixing help), but current options are fragmented across freelancer marketplaces, DAW-local AI tools, and disconnected collaboration products.

## Target users

1. Indie artists releasing singles/EPs who need instant accompaniment ideas
2. Producers needing specialist instrumentalists/vocalists or fast arrangement drafts
3. Session musicians seeking repeat remote work on hybrid AI-plus-human productions
4. Songwriters who need playable piano/guitar parts before hiring full collaborators

## Value proposition

Sessiono provides a music-specific workflow: brief-based AI accompaniment generation + optional human session hiring + secure collaboration + milestone delivery in one place.

## Competitive landscape

- **SoundBetter (Spotify-owned)**: high-trust marketplace positioning for professional music services
- **AirGigs**: dedicated remote session musician marketplace
- **Fiverr**: broad freelance marketplace with large music-services category
- **AI bandmate / accompaniment tools**: growing class of products focused on instant arrangement generation inside or alongside music software

### Sessiono differentiation

- Music-first project brief structure (BPM/key/reference aware)
- Creator-friendly milestone workflow for stem-based deliveries
- Live AI session agents for first-pass piano, guitar, and accompaniment generation
- Human handoff path when creators want polish, overdubs, or stylistic refinement
- Trust and safety controls tuned for audio deliverables and revision cycles

## Market signals (sourced)

- IFPI reported global recorded music revenues grew **4.8% in 2024** with streaming representing the majority of revenues, indicating continued demand for digital music production/distribution workflows.
- Spotify publicly announced the acquisition of SoundBetter, validating demand for creator-service marketplaces tightly coupled to music ecosystems.
- 2025 AI music market coverage highlights continued growth in AI “bandmate” and accompaniment tooling, especially for prompt-driven ideation and arrangement workflows.
- 2025 music-business reporting emphasizes that attribution, licensing, and consent are becoming core purchase criteria for AI-assisted music products.

## Launch strategy

## Phase 1: Founder-led acquisition

- Direct outreach to 50 seed creators and 50 seed session musicians
- Demo the “upload draft -> get piano/guitar accompaniment ideas in minutes” workflow in onboarding
- Concierge onboarding with white-glove profile setup
- Collect case studies from first 20 completed projects

## Phase 2: Creator ecosystem integration

- Partnerships with producer communities, songwriting Discords, and indie label networks
- Content partnerships with educators showing how AI drafts can be upgraded by human session players
- Referral incentives for repeat collaboration teams

## Phase 3: Scaled growth

- SEO content around session pricing, remote recording prep, and collaboration checklists
- SEO and social around AI accompaniment for demos, toplining, and rehearsal prep
- Paid social targeted by genre communities and DAW interests

## KPIs

- Time-to-first-hire
- Time-to-first-accompaniment
- Brief-to-delivery completion rate
- 30-day repeat booking rate
- Gross services volume (GSV)
- Dispute rate per 100 projects
- AI draft-to-human conversion rate

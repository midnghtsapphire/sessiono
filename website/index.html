Process

How It Works

From idea to finished track in four simple steps

1

Post Your Track Brief

Share genre, BPM, key, references, budget, and instrumentation needs. Upload a demo if you have one. The more detail, the better the AI generation.

2

AI Generates Options

Live AI music agents generate piano, guitar, pad, and accompaniment options from your brief in seconds. Listen, compare, and select your favorites.

3

Refine with Humans

Review AI parts, request changes, or layer session musician performances over AI foundations. Book real musicians for vocals, solos, or final polish.

4

Export & Ship

Download stems, MIDI, or final mixes. Export to any DAW. Share with clients or ship directly to streaming platforms.
